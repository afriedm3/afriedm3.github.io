Subaru Engine Swap

After my 2017 Subaru Crosstrek broke down on my cross-country road trip home from school, and a friendly man in the parking lot at the O'riellys in Rawlins, Wyoming suggested I fixed the car myself, my father and I decided to complete our first ever engine swap.

After weeks of work, lots of trips to the auto parts store, and tons of youtube videos we were able to remove the old engine, swap over the accessories to the new engine, and put the new engine into the car.
