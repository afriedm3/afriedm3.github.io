About

For as long as I remember, I have always enjoyed learning by doing. At a young age, this manifested with legos and art. In middle school, for a school project I made a radar gun that transmitted to a phone via bluetooth. Finally, this love for hands-on learning strongly influenced my decision to study Physics and Data Science.

Throughout my studies, my biggest learning takeaways have come from the hands-on projects I have completed. During these projects I get to experiment and fail, then learn from these failures to grow. Thus, I decided to make this website to showcase some of my favorite projects I have done both in school and outside.
