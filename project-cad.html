Computer Aided Design

This project showcases my work with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, specifically using Onshape to create 3D models and technical designs. CAD is an essential tool in modern engineering, allowing for precise visualization and planning of mechanical components and assemblies.

Through this project, I developed skills in 3D modeling, technical drawing, and design thinking. The ability to translate ideas into detailed digital models is crucial for prototyping and manufacturing processes.

This work demonstrates the intersection of creativity and technical precision, showing how digital tools can be used to bring engineering concepts to life before physical production.
