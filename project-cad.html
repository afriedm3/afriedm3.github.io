Computer Aided Design

This project showcases my work with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software of Onshape. At Oberlin College, we have the opportunity to do Winter Term Projects. These are month long projects that take place in in the month of January when we do not have classes. For one of these projects I chose to teach myself computer aided design. Through this project, I gained skills in 3D modeling which allowed me to work at the intersection of my passions of art and engineering. I chose to use do this project using OnShape after consulting with mechanical engineers that suggested it as a free alternative with a similar user interface as AutoCad.
